Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the e.Cop application (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) handles user data. Our goal is to keep the App simple and secure while collecting only what is required for the service to function.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The e.Cop app does not collect or store:

  • Personal data such as name, email, address, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Browsing history
  • Advertisement or marketing-related data

2. Information We Collect

To provide essential service functionality, the App may collect the following:

  • User login information (login name) provided by your organization
  • Verification code (OTP) for login security

This data is used only for secure login and to ensure the App works for registered users. We do not use the data for analytics, advertising, or tracking.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All necessary authentication data is stored securely on our servers and is accessible only to authorized systems. No personal information is shared with any third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking frameworks.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any external parties. User information is used solely for providing access to the App.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. Authentication data is transmitted securely and stored using industry-standard methods.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for authorized users only and is not designed for children.
